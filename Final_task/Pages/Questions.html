Frequently Asked Questions

You can book your appointment online here or by writing an e-mail to info@NourishmentZone.lv. Please note if your health insurance has nutritionist consultations included in the program.
Yes, we accept patients from all major insurance companies. If you wish to book in with your insurance company, please e-mail us with your membership number at the time of booking.
If you require additional investigations or treatment (e.g. medications), we will write to your personal doctor or consultant. If you prefer, we can recommend a trusted private medical colleague. We don’t believe taking commission for referrals or ordering tests so you can rest assured that our recommendations will be based on the treatment that we believe is best for you.
If you have read the information about our dietitians and you’re not sure who will be right for you, please e-mail or call us and we be happy to advise you.
Initial Consultations are booked for up to 90 minutes and will include a full assessment of your medical history, lifestyle and dietary habits and we will reach a nutritional diagnosis. You will be sent a Nutritional Treatment Plan and any other resources you may need after the consultation. Follow-up consultations will involve reviewing your treatment and offering ongoing support in implementation or adaptation of your plan.
If will not attent the appointment, contact us by phone +371 24 057 167 or e-mail info@NourishmentZone.lv. We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations within 48 hours of the appointment will be charged at 100% of the cost of the appointment. We understand that many of our patients have complex medical needs, so if you have a medical emergency or a valid reason for cancelling/not attending, please contact us.
